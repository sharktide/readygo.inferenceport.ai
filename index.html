What ReadyGo Does

Every feature built
for the real road.

From the moment you start your engine, ReadyGo is analyzing your environment so you don't have to.

🟢
Green Light Alerts

Know the instant the light turns green before the car in front of you reacts. No more lost seconds, no more honking.

⏱
Live Countdowns

See exactly how many seconds until the light changes. Plan your approach, stay calm, and eliminate unnecessary braking.

⚠️
Real-Time Safety Alerts

Distracted driver ahead? Flash flooding detected? ReadyGo surfaces hazards before they become emergencies.

🏎
Speed Recommendations

ML-optimized speed suggestions help you hit the next light on green, reducing stop-and-go and saving fuel.

🧠
Adaptive ML Engine

ReadyGo learns your commute over time, personalizing its predictions to your routes and driving patterns.

🌧
Weather & Hazard Awareness

Live monitoring for road hazards, flash floods, and low-visibility conditions keeps every journey safer — not just faster.

How It Works

Simple to use.
Serious technology.

ReadyGo is a two-part system: a compact device that mounts in your car and a companion app on your phone.

1
Mount the ReadyGo Device

The ReadyGo sensor mounts easily to your windshield or dashboard. It pairs with your phone in under 60 seconds via Bluetooth. No tools, no wiring.

2
Machine Learning Takes Over

Our on-device ML model continuously processes camera input, traffic signal data, and GPS context to build a live picture of your surroundings.

3
The App Guides You

Real-time alerts, countdowns, and speed recommendations surface in the ReadyGo app — non-intrusive, glanceable, and designed for eyes-up driving.

4
You Drive Smarter

Less idling, fewer hard stops, fewer surprises. ReadyGo users save fuel, arrive calmer, and contribute to smoother traffic for everyone around them.

Our Story

Built for the
Community.

"Traffic, crashes, and flash flooding are extremely dangerous. Keeping everyone safe is not just our mission — it's our responsibility."

ReadyGo was born out of a simple frustration: the car in front isn't moving, and you have no idea why or for how long. But behind that frustration is a real problem — inattentive driving wastes time, wastes fuel, and costs lives.

We're a team of innovators building technology that makes driving comfortable and intuitive for everyone — from the seasoned commuter to the brand-new driver navigating their first rush hour.
